### AI 시대의 세탁소 만들기 연습 - 간단한 비행기 게임 만들어보기

```jsx
"Create a 3D arcade-style flight shooter game using JavaScript and the Three.js library (via CDN).

0. Branding & Copyright (Crucial):
   - Add a comment block at the very top of the code: 'Project: Neon Sky Ace | Created by Jay | AI City Builders & Connect AI LAB'.
   - In the game UI, add a small, semi-transparent footer text centered at the bottom of the screen that says: "© AI City Builders & Connect AI LAB by Jay". Use a futuristic font.

1. Setup & Environment:
   - Initialize a Three.js scene with a perspective camera and a WebGL renderer.
   - Aesthetic: 'Vaporwave' style. Deep purple fog, a glowing endless grid floor that moves backwards to create the illusion of forward speed, and a starry background.
   - Lighting: Ambient light and a directional light to cast shadows.

2. The Player (The Jet):
   - Create a sleek, futuristic jet using a group of simple geometries (e.g., a ConeGeometry for the body, thin Boxes for wings).
   - Color: Cyan/Blue neon material.
   - Controls: The jet is locked to the bottom-center initially but follows the mouse cursor's X and Y position to steer. It should bank (tilt) slightly left/right when moving.

3. The Enemies:
   - Generate neon Red cubes or tetrahedrons that spawn at random X/Y positions far in the distance (negative Z).
   - They should move towards the player (positive Z) at a constant speed.
   - Add simple collision detection: If the player hits an enemy, game over.

4. The Weapon:
   - The player fires yellow laser beams (thin glowing cylinders) when the mouse is clicked (or auto-fire).
   - Lasers move fast into the distance.
   - If a laser hits an enemy, destroy both the enemy and the laser, spawn a simple particle explosion (a few small squares flying out), and increase the Score.

5. UI:
   - Add a minimalist HUD overlay in HTML/CSS showing the 'SCORE' in the top-left corner.
   - Ensure the Copyright Footer (from step 0) is always visible."
```
